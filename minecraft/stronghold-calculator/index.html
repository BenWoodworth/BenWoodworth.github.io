    How to use

    Open the debug screen with the F3 key. Throw an eye of ender, line up the green on the crosshairs with the center of the eye of ender when it's at its highest, and copy your x, z, and yaw into the calculator.

    Move a few hundred blocks to the left or right (perpendicular to the direction of the first eye of ender), then throw another, and copy the coordinates to the calculator. The yaw should be different by at least a few degrees (the greater the difference, the better).
